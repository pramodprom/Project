✦ Core Features

Everything you need to
understand your people

From org charts to deep data analytics, Nexus gives HR and IT leaders the full picture.

Org Explorer
Browse your entire organization with smart filtering, column customization, and instant search across 30+ employee attributes.
Hierarchy Mapping
Visualize upward chains and full org trees. See the complete reporting structure from any employee to the CEO.
Rich Profiles
Deep employee profiles with job history, contact details, department info, account status — all pulled live from Microsoft 365.
Export Anywhere
Download org data as CSV or Excel with one click. Export hierarchy views as high-res images for presentations.
Smart Caching
Background prefetching loads your entire org on login. Switch between pages with zero wait — data is always ready.
Enterprise Secure
OAuth 2.0 via Microsoft identity platform. No passwords stored. Token-based sessions with automatic refresh.
